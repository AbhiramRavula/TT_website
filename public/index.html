Our Mission

  • To encourage students to participate in world-class competitions.
  • To educate enthusiastic students in learning design and development.
  • To enrich student's programming practices by inculcating industry-wide coding style.

Our Vision

  • To foster development of technical and technological skills amongst students in various aspects of programming and hardware, make them aware of industry ready practices used in the field of CS and IT

Events by us

Here at Tech Tycoons, we offer a wide range of events to build technical and communication skills amongst our participants.

These events also establish industry-ready skills amongst our audience.

A sneak-peak at a couple of events organized by us include:

1) Technical Quiz

Quizzes would be designed to cultivate student's mind by asking inquisitive questionnaires

We will test student's problem solving and theoretical knowledge in tech

These would be conducted in increasing order of difficulty

2) Seminars

Seminars would be geared towards a wide range of IT domains

This would introduce applied-programming practices to make them ready for the real experiences.

3) Hackathons

Hackathons would be organized to improve collaboration abilities of students

Hackathons will serve as a medium to introduce cutting edge technologies to students and make them industry-ready

4)Coding competitions

Coding competitions are organized to boost student's problem solving abilities

This would prepare them to face world-class coding competitions like ICPC, Google CodeJam, Meta HackerCup.

5) Paper Presentations

Paper presentations will be organized to aid innovative thinking in students

With a number of themes to choose from, paper presentations teach the process of innovation to students.
